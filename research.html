English Version|中文版

Research Interests

    I am mainly interested in PDEs of fluids. In specific, I'm focusing on:
  • Free-boundary problem of (mostly compressible) fluids (with or without surface tension) and corresponding vortex sheet/contact discontinuity problems, e.g., water wave, relativistic fluid, MHD, elastodynamics, etc.
    • Other topics that I am interested in (but not in research currently)
  • The formation, stability and evolution of singularities in compressible fluids.
  • Long time solution of (compressible) fluids, e.g., small data long time wellposedness, stability of certain equilibria.
