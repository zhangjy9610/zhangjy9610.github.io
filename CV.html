Research Interest
    I am mainly interested in PDEs of fluids. In specific, I'm focusing on:
  • Free-boundary problem of MHD equations with or without surface tension (wellposedness, etc);
  • Free-boundary problem of water wave equations with or without surface tension (wellposedness, long time behaviour, etc);
  • Counterexamples of (low regularity) Illposedness of compressible fluid equations.
