Research Interest
    I am mainly interested in PDEs of fluids. In specific, I'm focusing on:
  • Free-boundary problem of (mostly compressible) fluids (with or without surface tension) and corresponding vortex sheet problems, e.g., water wave, relativistic liquid, and complex fluids: MHD, elastodynamics, liquid crystal, etc.
    • Other topics that I am interested in (but not in research currently)
  • Low regularity illposedness/Shock/Singularity formation of fluids or wave equations.
